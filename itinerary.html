Road trip

Iconic sunrise at the lake then drive to Utah

Stay: Hoodoo Moab Hotel · or Element Moab as alternative
  • 6:00 AM★Maroon Bells shuttle at opening (~$8 from Aspen Highlands) — lake reflection at sunrise

Road trip
Moab — Arches & Dead Horse Point
Red rock arches, canyon overlooks — a full Utah day
Stay: Hoodoo Moab Hotel (2nd night)
  • 7:00 AM★Arches National Park — Delicate Arch Trail (3 mi, 480 ft gain): iconic arch at sunrise
  • 10:00 AMWindows Section: Double Arch, North & South Windows

Day10
Road trip
Moab → Black Canyon of the Gunnison
Long scenic drive + canyon — via Million Dollar Highway if desired
Stay: Hampton Inn Montrose · or TownePlace Suites as alternative
  • 8:00 AMDepart Moab → Montrose (3h30 via US-191 N + US-50 W) — or Million Dollar Highway option

Day11
Road trip
Black Canyon → Great Sand Dunes
Morning canyon, afternoon drive to the dunes
Stay: Hampton Inn Alamosa · or Great Sand Dunes campground
  • 8:00 AM★Black Canyon NP: Painted Wall View, Warner Point Nature Trail (2 mi)
  • 11:00 AMDepart Montrose → Great Sand Dunes NP (2h30 via US-285 S)
  • 1:30 PMArrive — rent sandboard (~$20) at park entrance
  • 2:00 PM★Climb High Dune (850 ft gain, ~2.5h on loose sand)
  • 4:30 PMSandboard the dunes · Medano Creek wading
  • 6:00 PMDrive to Alamosa (~45 min) · dinner at San Luis Valley Brewing Co.
Tips Painted Wall: best in morning light. Sand Dunes: surface >150°F (65°C) after 1 PM — start climb no later than 2 PM, bring 3L+ water.
